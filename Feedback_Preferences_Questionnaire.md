## How to Use This Questionnaire

### For Leaders:

1. **Complete your own questionnaire** first - model vulnerability
2. **Share your preferences** with your team
3. **Have each team member** complete their own
4. **Discuss as a team** - learn from each other
5. **Keep preferences accessible** - reference before feedback conversations
6. **Update annually** - preferences can change

### For Teams:

1. Schedule a team session (60-90 minutes)
2. Have everyone complete the questionnaire beforehand
3. Share highlights in round-robin format
4. Discuss commonalities and differences
5. Create a "Feedback Preferences" document for the team
6. Reference before giving feedback to colleagues

### For Personal Relationships:

1. Complete together with partner/family member
2. Discuss each section openly
3. Note key differences
4. Practice new approaches
5. Check in periodically on what's working
