## Part 5: 90-Day Milestones

### Week 1-2: Awareness
- [ ] Complete this action plan
- [ ] Identify top 2-3 fixed mindset behaviors to address
- [ ] Share mindset concepts with one person

### Week 3-4: Feedback Foundation
- [ ] Complete Feedback Preferences questionnaire myself
- [ ] Have feedback preference conversations with 3+ people
- [ ] Give informal feedback to at least 5 people

### Month 2: Experimentation
- [ ] Launch one personal experiment
- [ ] Create one safe-to-fail opportunity for my team
- [ ] Celebrate one "learning failure" (mine or team's)

### Month 3: Integration
- [ ] Review and update this action plan
- [ ] Measure progress on behavior changes
- [ ] Share success story with peer group

## Part 6: Trigger Phrases

### When I Hear Myself Say... | Replace With...

| Fixed Mindset Phrase | Growth Mindset Alternative |
|----------------------|---------------------------|
| "I'm not good at this" | "I'm not good at this **yet**" |
| "This is too hard" | "This will take some time and effort" |
| "I made a mistake" | "Mistakes help me learn" |
| "I can't do it" | "I can't do it **yet**" |
| "It's good enough" | "Is this my best work?" |
| "That person is so talented" | "I wonder what they did to develop that skill" |
| "Feedback hurts" | "Feedback is a gift" |
| "I already know this" | "What can I learn from this?" |
